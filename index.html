Who I Am

About Me

I am a dedicated final-year law student at SASTRA University with a keen interest in Public Policy and legal research. With a strong foundation in taxation law, arbitration, intellectual property, and commercial law, I bring both theoretical understanding and practical experience.

My internship journey spans across some of India's reputed law firms and chambers, giving me exposure to diverse practice areas including GST litigation, electricity regulation, arbitration, and civil matters. I am passionate about contributing meaningfully to the legal profession through continuous learning and hands-on experience.

Beyond academics, I communicate complex legal concepts through social media to bridge the gap between law and the general public — a reflection of my belief that legal literacy is a civic necessity.
